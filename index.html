What is SoCraTes Day Paris?

SoCraTes Day is an International Conference for Software Craft and Testing, a single-day version of the full SoCraTes Conferences located in Paris.
It provides an ideal setting for speaking to and coding with other talented and engaged developers, letting us learn from and with each other.
Information on what to expect, how to prepare, and on previous years’ events can be found in our wiki.
To get an impression of what kind of topics were discussed and workshops were initiated, take a look into the photo gallery from last year. Also, get on the mailing list!

What it's all about ?

What it's all about

This event is about the sustainable creation of useful software in a responsible way.

It’s about People

Everyone who is concerned with coding, testing, code quality, software craftsmanship.

It’s about Action

The event will be run as a non-profit, low-cost event. We will be there for some hours to collaborate and share ideas.

It’s about Fun

There will be much to do during these few hours !
